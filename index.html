About Isomera

Isomera is building on our prior work developing generative models for drug design, extending it toward a true world model for biology — trained, validated, and running today. We use a two-model generative approach to identify correct scaffolds and orient molecular components, developing an intuition for biophysical binding that generalizes beyond existing data. Our goal is a system that understands biology deeply enough to design drugs from first principles, rather than relying on pattern-matching alone.
