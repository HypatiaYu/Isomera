Why we need better ways to design and test treatments for PTSD

⚡

PTSD's Persistent Impact

PTSD drives persistent fight-or-flight activation and flashbacks that disrupt attention, causing behavior to appear scattered and reactive. Even after recovery, it's hard to prove improvement.

📊

The Clinical Disconnect

Clinical tools measure simplified, isolated tasks in controlled settings. Real-world cognition is high-dimensional, context-dependent, and plays out across dynamic 3D environments.

🎯

The Gap

High-dimensional cognition measured by low-dimensional tests. We bridge this gap by measuring functional cognitive outputs directly in immersive 3D worlds.

What We've Built

A Generative Model for Drug Discovery: A working generative model trained, validated, and running today—built by teaching software to "draw" molecules the way artists learn to draw. The system builds biophysical intuition by learning from real molecular interactions.

Brain-Targeted Therapeutics: We are partnering with Chromologic to develop compounds for traumatic brain injury. These compounds will be synthesized and tested in mouse models for TBI efficacy.

Direct Measurement Platform: We're building immersive 3D environments to measure functional cognitive outputs directly—not signal inference. We measure how people actually behave, think, and function in complex, high-dimensional worlds that mirror real life.
